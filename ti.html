Farmer Project

An agriculture website that is dedicated to empowering farmers with the knowledge that they need through the power of technology. This will act as a pathway of virtually connecting farmers to other farmers, consumers/customers, and the government. It is also a portal for farmers to get a single view of their policy and get claim support. Through this a range of services that farmers across India can use to help aid their farming efforts will be provided. Click here for contact list in case of queries.
